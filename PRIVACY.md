# Privacy Policy for Matcha Chrome Extension

**Last updated: February 9, 2026**

## Overview

Matcha is a Chrome extension that helps job seekers match their CV/resume against job descriptions using AI analysis, and track their job applications. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

## 1. Data We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **CV/Resume text**: The text content of your uploaded CV or resume
- **Job tracker data**: Job titles, company names, URLs, application status, and notes you add

### Information from Google Sign-In
- Email address
- Display name
- Profile picture URL

### Automatically Collected
- Job posting content from web pages you choose to analyze

## 2. How We Use Your Data

- **CV text**: Sent to our secure backend server for AI-powered analysis against job descriptions
- **Google account info**: Used solely for authentication and displaying your profile
- **Job tracker data**: Stored locally to help you manage and track your job applications
- **Job posting content**: Analyzed in combination with your CV to provide match scores and tailored suggestions

## 3. Data Storage

- **Local Storage**: Your CV text, job tracker data, and preferences are stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API
- **Authentication tokens**: Stored locally on your device
- **Server-side**: We do not permanently store your CV or job posting content on our servers. Data is processed for analysis and immediately discarded

## 4. Data Sharing

- **We do not sell your data** to third parties
- **AI Analysis**: CV and job posting text is sent to our backend which uses Azure OpenAI for analysis. Microsoft's Azure OpenAI does not retain customer data for model training
- **No advertising**: We do not share data with advertisers

## 5. Data Security

- All communications between the extension and our servers use HTTPS encryption
- Authentication is handled through secure Google OAuth 2.0
- We follow industry-standard security practices

## 6. Your Rights and Controls

You have full control over your data:

- **Delete CV**: Remove your stored CV anytime via the extension settings
- **Clear Tracker**: Delete all job tracking data from settings
- **Sign Out**: Remove your authentication data by signing out
- **Uninstall**: Removing the extension deletes all locally stored data

## 7. Third-Party Services

This extension uses:
- **Google OAuth**: For user authentication ([Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy))
- **Azure OpenAI**: For AI-powered CV analysis ([Microsoft Privacy Statement](https://privacy.microsoft.com/en-us/privacystatement))

## 8. Children's Privacy

This extension is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date.
